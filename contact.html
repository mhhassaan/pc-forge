About PCForge

PCForge is an educational platform designed to help users understand PC components and build their dream computers. Whether you're a gaming enthusiast, content creator, or professional, our tools make it easy to design, compare, and save custom PC builds.

Our mission is to democratize PC building knowledge and make it accessible to everyone, from beginners to experienced builders.

Key Features

  • Smart PC Builder with real-time pricing
  • Component comparison tools
  • Educational hardware guides
  • Pre-built configuration gallery
  • Save and manage your builds
  • User authentication system
